About Us

LogicNifty is a free educational platform created to assist traders and investors with technical insights into the Indian stock market. Our mission is to simplify signal-based trading using automated logic and real-time data.

Who We Are

We are a team of passionate market enthusiasts and developers with experience in building automation tools, market scanners, and real-time dashboards. LogicNifty was built as a side project to share these tools freely with the trading community.

What We Do

We use open data from NSE India and optionally Upstox APIs to analyze market patterns, breakout signals, bullish/bearish movements, and option chain behavior. All tools are algorithmic, updated in real-time using automation scripts and Firebase.

Our Vision

To make data-driven trading education accessible to everyone. We aim to empower independent traders with simplified dashboards, actionable insights, and transparent logic — without noise or hype.

Support the Project

LogicNifty is funded by voluntary donations. If you find our tools useful, consider supporting us via the donation section on the homepage. Every contribution helps keep LogicNifty free and live!
